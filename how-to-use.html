WordCompass has several toggles to customize your search experience. The most common toggles are in the Preferences menu (‚öôÔ∏è gear icon):

Synonyms ‚Üî Antonyms

Switch between finding similar words or opposite words.

"good" ‚Üí Antonyms: bad, terrible, awful

Thesaurus ‚Üî Creative Phrases

Single words vs. multi-word expressions and phrases.

"money" ‚Üí Thesaurus: cash, currency, funds
"money" ‚Üí Creative Phrases: financial resources, economic capital

Switch between quick results or more thoughtful suggestions.

Show just words or include brief definitions.

With Definitions: happy (feeling pleasure), joyful (full of joy), elated (in high spirits)
